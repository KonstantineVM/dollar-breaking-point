    WHO WE ARE

    China Investment Corporation (CIC), founded on 29 September 2007, was established as a vehicle to diversify China's foreign exchange holdings and seek maximum returns for its shareholder within acceptable risk tolerance. Three subsidiaries of CIC—CIC International Co., Ltd. (CIC International), CIC Capital Corporation (CIC Capital) and Central Huijin Investment Ltd. (Central Huijin)—were incorporated separately and function as three distinct entities.

    WHAT WE DO

    As a financial investor, we are guided by the principle of long-term investment and risk diversification.
